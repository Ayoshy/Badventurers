# Vision

Last updated: 2026-06-21

## One-Liner

Badventurers is an idle incremental RPG where the player manages a fantasy guild full of underqualified adventurers who somehow keep bringing back loot.

## Promise

Open the app, send a bad party on a worse quest, get funny logs and useful loot, upgrade the guild, and feel clever for turning incompetence into profit.

## Pillars

1. **Fast idle loop**: meaningful progress in under a minute, with offline rewards for return sessions.
2. **Absurd RPG fantasy**: familiar dungeon-crawling ingredients, but filtered through bureaucracy, cowardice, poor equipment, and accidental heroism.
3. **Loot joy**: frequent rewards, funny item names, obvious upgrades, and enough rarity sparkle to keep runs sticky.
4. **Ethical monetization**: rewarded ads and purchases help or decorate; they do not make the free game feel broken.
5. **Bilingual by design**: English and French are first-class content targets, with adapted humor rather than literal translation.

## Audience

- Mobile players who like idle, incremental, gacha-adjacent reward loops, and RPG progression.
- Fantasy fans who enjoy parody and character-driven item flavor.
- Players who want a casual second-screen game, not a demanding action game.

## Positioning

Badventurers sits between an idle management game and a comedic RPG. The player is not a chosen hero. The player is the poor soul trying to make the guild profitable despite its members.

## North Star

Players should want to reopen the app because they are curious what their party did this time and because one more upgrade feels reachable.
