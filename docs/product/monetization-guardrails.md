# Monetization Guardrails

Last updated: 2026-06-22

This note turns the competitive research into concrete rules for future ad and store work.

- Rewarded ads stay optional and never gate core progression.
- No pay-to-win, no loot boxes, and no randomized paid rewards.
- Paid paths stay limited to clear-value offers like no-ads, cosmetics, and starter packs.
- Fake rewarded-ad prompts always keep the normal collect path equally visible.
- Debug and prototype ad flows may speed up timers or double rewards only outside production builds.
- Future ad work should link back here and to the competitive research note.
