# Common Loot Art Briefs

Source scope: `docs/data/items.csv`, `docs/content/hero-item-catalog.md`, and `docs/design/art-direction.md`.

Purpose: reviewed-ready briefs for the 25 existing Common loot items. These are for art production or image-generation passes only; they do not replace the item catalog.

Common art target: polished 16-bit-inspired pixel icons with chunky silhouettes, warm/cool lighting contrast, restrained rarity treatment, and enough absurd fantasy detail to read before the player reads the item name. Avoid readable text in the art.

## Production Checklist

- Each item must be recognizable at phone UI size before color or frame is considered.
- Items sharing a slot must not reuse the same base shape, pose, or trim arrangement.
- Common rarity should feel humble, used, and funny, but still polished.
- The comedy hook should be visible in the object itself, not only in the item name.

## Briefs

### weapon_bent_spoon - Bent Spoon

- Item: `weapon_bent_spoon`; display name: Bent Spoon; slot: Weapon; rarity: Common.
- Distinct silhouette: Short spoon held like a tiny rapier, with an oversized oval bowl, a lazy S-bend handle, and a chipped pointed end.
- Materials/colors: Tarnished pewter, one dull brass repair band, pale gravy stain in the bowl, cool blue shadow on the bent edge.
- Comedy/Badventurers hook: A tavern utensil promoted to weapon duty, still greasy but posed with unearned drama.
- Mobile readability note: Make the bowl and handle bend the two big reads; avoid thin cutlery-line proportions at 48dp.
- Forbidden shortcuts: No normal sword, no plain spoon silhouette, no abstract diagonal stick, no readable label, no generic gold diamond placeholder.
